Wordle(Ty's Version)

For the final project of my C#/.NET class, I chose to make my own version of Wordle. The main reason why I chose to make a version of Wordle was because right when Wordle came out, I had a lot of my friends ask me if I could make my own version of it and around the same time my teacher said we could make anything we wanted for our final project, so I thought that this was the perfect chance to try it out. I made Wordle entirely out of C# and Visual Studio and I added my own touches. I made it so you could play again without having to wait twenty-four hours and I added the ability to see the amount of right and wrong guesses. The project was a joy to work on and I was really happy with the end result.
